🌙 Featured Creative Project: Moon & Venus

This project combines my love for art, poetry and astronomy-inspired storytelling. It tells the story of the Moon, who eternally longs for Venus. Hidden by the brilliance of the Sun, Venus remains beyond reach during the day. The Moon patiently waits for sunset, hoping for a brief glimpse of her presence. It is a story about pure, eternal and unwavering love.

Moon's Monologue

ill pray in winter ,summer, spring and autumn..
and my hope of your arrival
in amidst of cricket's song & firefly's dance
ill wait all day till the sun goes down
when your first glimpse of love shine..
you'll be the only spark i see
just you and me
close enough to grasp your beauty
what a beautiful cruel curse it is
to be close enough but not reach you.

when sun hides you in his bright blanket,
when the horizon swallow you whole,
i can only count numbers till you return...
even if the world keeps us apart
my eternal devotion and soul
will revolve around you
guide me to your light

( because eres mi luna )
